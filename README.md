Indus Global Exports Website
This is a fully responsive, modern, and professional website designed for Indus Global Exports, an international trading and export company.

Features:

Home Page: Hero section with headline, subheading, and call-to-action highlighting global reach and reliability.

About Page: Company history, mission, vision, and commitment to quality.

Products We Export Page: Showcases exported products with images, descriptions, and categories.

Contact Page: Contact form, email, phone, and office location.

Testimonials Page: Client feedback with names and photos.

Terms & Conditions Page: Company policies, trade terms, and legal disclaimers.

Technologies Used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

Fully responsive for desktop, tablet, and mobile

Professional typography and layout

Interactive UI elements and smooth hover animations

Purpose:
This website serves as an online presence for Indus Global Exports, helping the company showcase its products, build trust with international clients, and streamline communication.
